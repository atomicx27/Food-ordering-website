Order Food in Trains Online through APS Services


Rely on pantry car food to quell your hunger? Not anymore, those days are long gone. Now, you can order food in trains online and choose among multiple food options while on route. To supply meals aboard trains, APS Services has teamed up with a licenced catering partner of IRCTC & RailRestro.


We work with full diligence to deliver fresh and delicious cuisine to passengers at their seat.


We serve a range of cuisines, including North Indian, Chinese, Italian, Jain food, Mughlai, South Indian, and Continental. You won't miss your favourite cuisines when travelling because regional foods are furthermore offered together with these options. Don't wait, order NOW!




Why Order Food Online from APS Services?


Order food online through IRCTC's e-catering partners instead of eating out of your car's pantry. The absence of sanitation in pantry food has been demonstrated by recent allegations from well-known food safety and security organisations as well as mounting complaints.


Now you can order food in advance with APS Services since you will receive delectable and fresh food from top restaurants delivered right to your seat when your train arrives at the station. Our top-notch, FSSAI-approved eateries prepare the food. They adhere to the rules and prepare food in a sanitary and orderly kitchen. The cuisine is excellent and nutritious to consume while travelling.




Order Food in Trains Online through APS Services


STEP 1: Visit the official website of RailMitra – www.railmitra.com


STEP 2: Go to the “Food in Train” option on the website.


STEP 3: You can choose to either “Order via PNR” by entering your 10-digit PNR number of “Order via Train No.” by entering your Train Name or Number along with the Boarding Date.


STEP 4: Select the Boarding Stations where you want to receive your food on the train.


STEP 5: A list of restaurants and menu will appear from where you can choose your favourite food items.


STEP 6: Pay online by Credit card, Debit card, Net Banking or digital wallets or choose “Cash on Delivery.”


STEP 7: Relax and wait at your seat where your food order will be delivered on-time.
